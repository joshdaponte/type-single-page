History

Fraunces is a contemporary serif typeface designed by Flavia Zimbardi and Phaedra Charles. It started development in 2018 and was released in 2020 through Undercase Type. The design pulls from early 20th-century display typography, like bold, expressive serif styles commonly seen in old advertising and editorial headlines. It’s classified as a display serif, and meant for larger uses where personality and impact is the intention. Fraunces stands out due to its variable font system, where properties like weight, softness, optical size, and contrast can be adjusted, shifting from sharp and high-contrast to soft and more rounded. The typeface’s practicality comes from its ability to support a wide range of Latin-based languages. Fraunces is designed to balance historical influence with modern flexibility, which is why it is suitable for distinctive branding, web design, and editorial layouts.
